Cheeseburger

Description

A quintessential American food, responsible for shortening the lives of many cows and humans.

Ingredients

  • vehicle or smartphone
  • money

Steps

  1. Get in vehicle OR skip to step 9.
  2. Drive vehicle to a fast-food restaurant with a drive-thru window.
  3. Order a cheeseburger at the drive-thru window, making sure to act like a decent (albeit lazy) human being during all your dealings with the restaurant staff. They are working hard to support your laziness.
  4. Pay money for the cheeseburger.
  5. Receive the cheeseburger.
  6. Drive your vehicle back home, being careful not to lose the cheeseburger along the way.
  7. Unwrap the cheeseburger.
  8. Eat the cheeseburger
  9. ALTERNATIVE to steps 1-8: Use smartphone to access the app for Uber Eats, Doordash, or another food delivery service.
  10. Order and pay for a cheeseburger.
  11. Receive the cheeseburger and tip the delivery driver.
  12. Unwrap the cheeseburger.
  13. Eat the cheeseburger.
