Canned Chili

Description

Canned chili is a great easy-prep meal for the lazy Odinite (or at least the non-vegetarian lazy Odinite). It's relatively tasty, quite filling, and a decent source of protein and iron.

Ingredients

  • ~15 oz. canned chili (brand of your choice)
  • can-opener (some cans come with this built in)
  • spoon
  • bowl (optional)
  • microwave (optional)

Steps

  1. Procure the ingredients, especially the canned chili.
  2. Open the can.
  3. For bonus lazy-points, eat the chili directly from the can with a spoon at room temperature. Otherwise, for normal laziness, skip to the next step.
  4. Pour the chili into the bowl, using the spoon to get it all out of the can.
  5. Place the bowl of chili into the microwave (DO NOT include the can or spoon, as putting out fires is not very lazy).
  6. Heat the chili in the microwave for 1 minute or to desired temperature.
  7. Remove bowl of chili from the microwave.
  8. Eat the chili from the bowl using the spoon.
