Chicken Tortilla Things

Description

Is it a taco? A wrap? A burrito? I don't know and I'm too lazy to figure it out, but it's quick, tasty, and filling!

Ingredients

  • 2 or 3 flour tortillas
  • frozen chicken strips
  • shredded cheese (optional)
  • other toppings as desired
  • 2 plates
  • microwave

Steps

  1. Put some frozen chicken strips on a plate (measurements shmeasurements).
  2. Place the plate of frozen chicken in the microwave and heat for 2 minutes or to desired temperature.
  3. While the chicken is heating, fold 2 or 3 flour tortillas and position them on a plate.
  4. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded cheese along the inner folds of the tortillas.
  5. Remove the frozen chicken from the microwave when it is no longer frozen and is at the desired temperature.
  6. Place the frozen chicken strips in the inner folds of the tortillas on top of the cheese, being careful to not stuff so full that the tortillas can no longer fit around their contents.
  7. Place the plate of tortillas, cheese, and chicken into the microwave and heat for 15-45 seconds, until the cheese is at the desired level of meltedness.
  8. Remove from the microwave.
  9. Add other toppings as desired.
  10. Wrap the tortillas around the good stuff and enjoy!
